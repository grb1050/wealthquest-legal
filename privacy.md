## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Wealth Quest, you may voluntarily provide:
- A **display name** (optional) for the leaderboard
- **In-app purchase information** processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play (we do not collect or store payment card details)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:
- **Device information**: device model, operating system version, language, time zone, and unique device identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS or AAID on Android, where permitted by you)
- **Usage data**: levels completed, in-app actions, session length, crash reports, and feature usage
- **Technical data**: IP address (truncated or anonymized where possible), app version, and network information

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your data on our behalf:
- **Firebase** (Google) — analytics, crash reporting, and remote configuration
- **Sentry** — error monitoring and crash diagnostics
- **AdMob** (Google) — in-app advertising
- **RevenueCat** — subscription and in-app purchase management
- **Apple App Store / Google Play** — app distribution and purchase processing

Each provider has its own privacy policy; we encourage you to review them.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- **Operate the App** — let you sign in, save your progress, and use core features
- **Improve gameplay** — analyze how players engage with levels so we can fix bugs and balance the game
- **Personalize your experience** — remember your name, preferences, and progress across sessions
- **Communicate with you** — send in-app notifications about new content or features (you can disable these in your device settings)
- **Show advertisements** — serve relevant ads in the App (only to users who consent and are over 13)
- **Process purchases** — handle in-app purchases and subscriptions through Apple or Google
- **Prevent fraud and abuse** — detect cheating, automated play, or violations of our Terms of Use
- **Comply with legal obligations** — respond to lawful requests and protect our rights

## 4. How We Share Your Information

**We do not sell your personal information.** We share information only in these limited situations:

- **With service providers** (Firebase, Sentry, AdMob, RevenueCat) acting on our behalf, under contractual confidentiality obligations
- **With advertising partners** (only aggregated or device-identifier data, only where you consented, and only if you are 13 or older)
- **For legal reasons** — to comply with subpoenas, court orders, or applicable law; to enforce our Terms of Use; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ourselves or others
- **In a business transaction** — if we are acquired, merged, or sell assets, your information may transfer to the successor entity
- **With your explicit consent** for any other purpose
