## Frequently Asked Questions

### Gameplay

**How do I play Wealth Quest?**
Match 3 or more matching tiles in a row or column to earn coins. Use those coins to invest in stocks, ETFs, and savings accounts — your holdings grow over time, even when you're not playing. Complete levels to unlock new investment types and city districts.

**Where can I see my progress over time?**
Tap **📈 View Wealth Chart** on the Home screen to see your wealth history, your all-time high, and how your current wealth compares to your peak.

**What do the colored tiles mean?**
- 💰 **Money** — earns coins when matched
- 📊 **ETF** — earns coins and supports diversification
- 🚀 **Stock** — higher reward, higher risk
- ⭐ **Bonus** — multipliers
- 💥 **Risk** — careful, these appear in later levels

### Purchases & Premium Pass

**I bought coins but they didn't appear in my account.**
First, try restoring purchases:

1. Open Wealth Quest
2. Go to the Store screen
3. Tap **"Restore Purchases"**
4. Wait a few seconds — they should appear

If they still don't show up, email us with:
- Your Apple ID email
- The approximate time of the purchase
- The amount charged (or a screenshot of the receipt)

**How do I cancel my Premium Pass subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed through Apple, not through Wealth Quest:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find **Wealth Quest** in the list
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

Your Premium benefits remain active until the end of your current billing period.

**Can I get a refund?**
Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us directly. To request one:

1. Go to [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find the Wealth Quest purchase
4. Tap **Report a Problem**
5. Choose a reason and submit

Apple decides on refunds case by case.

### Account & Progress

**Will I lose my progress if I delete the app?**
Your gameplay progress is saved locally on your device. If you delete the app, the local save is removed. Your **in-app purchases** (Premium Pass, Remove Ads, coin packs) can be restored on the same Apple ID using the **Restore Purchases** button — but your levels, wealth, and holdings will reset on a fresh install.

**Can I sync my progress between devices?**
Not at this time. Your progress stays on the device you're playing on.

### Ads

**There are too many ads — can I remove them?**
Yes. Go to the **Store** screen and purchase **"Remove Ads"** as a one-time in-app purchase. Banner and interstitial ads will be removed permanently for that Apple ID.

You can still choose to watch optional rewarded ads (the "Double Coins" button on Level Complete) — those stay available because they're entirely optional and give you a bonus.

**An ad froze the game.**
Close the app fully (swipe up from the bottom and flick Wealth Quest away) and reopen. If it keeps happening, email us with your device model and we'll investigate.

### Technical

**The app crashes when I open it.**

1. Force-close and reopen
2. If it's still crashing, restart your iPhone
3. If it's still crashing, try uninstalling and reinstalling (note: this will reset local progress, but in-app purchases can be restored)
4. If it's *still* crashing after all that, email us with your device model and iOS version

**A level seems impossible.**
Most levels have a clear path with enough moves, but some difficulty curves can feel sharp. If you're truly stuck:

- Use boosters from the Store
- Watch an optional rewarded ad for help
- If you think a specific level might be bugged, email us with the level number

### Privacy & Data

**What data do you collect?**
See our [Privacy Policy](https://grb1050.github.io/wealthquest-legal/privacy) for the full breakdown. Short version: device ID, gameplay events, crash reports, and ad/purchase data — all standard for free-to-play games. We do not collect real names, contacts, or location.

**Can I delete my data?**
Gameplay data is stored locally on your device — uninstall the app to delete it. For any data we hold on our analytics servers, email us and we'll process a deletion request within 30 days.

### Age & Content

**Is this game appropriate for kids?**
Wealth Quest is rated **4+** in the App Store. It contains no violence, profanity, or mature content. The investing concepts are introduced in age-appropriate language, but younger players may want a parent's help with strategy.

**Is this real financial advice?**
No. Wealth Quest is a game for entertainment and education. The concepts taught (diversification, compound growth, etc.) are real, but the game is not a substitute for licensed financial advice. Always do your own research before making real-world financial decisions.
